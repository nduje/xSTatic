Graffiti art is considered the greatest art movement since the Renaissance.


In addition to writing different styles of the alphabet, the characteristic of graffiti culture is its responsiveness to current events, where graffiti culture reacts much faster and stronger to the event or situation than all other media.


xSTatic festival hosts graffiti artists from all over the world and every year strives to present an attractive line-up, which, in addition to "style writers", always presents "mural" artists.

Although sports such as MTB and BMX, Inline skates and freestyle football were presented throughout the festival, only Skateboarding and Street-Baskeball are the disciplines in which competitors compete each year.


During the xSTatic festival, competitions in "Downhill" and "Sk8 course comeptition" were held in the skateboard section. Skaters are currently competing in the "Game of skate" discipline.

Although sports such as MTB and BMX, Inline skates and freestyle football were presented throughout the festival, only Skateboarding and Street-Baskeball are the disciplines in which competitors compete each year.


Street-basketball competes in the style of 3vs3.

Break-dance is an acrobatic dance that consists of 4 basic parts: toprock, downrock, power moves and freezes. With its dance moves that evoke enthusiasm, it is always a very accompanying part of the xSTatic festival, with loud cheering.


xSTatic festival has so far hosted world champions such as Pokemon Crew and Top9, as well as a large number of domestic and foreign B-Boy groups.

Like graffiti, rapping and DJing are basic elements of hip-hop culture, and the xSTatic festival always hosts rap artists and hip-hop DJs in its program.


Roots Manuva, Edo Maajka, TBF, Atheist Rap, Elemental, Frankie, Kandžija, Dječaci, Vojko, Grše, Krešo Bengalka, Ajs Nigrutin, Prti BeeGee, WhoSee Klapa and many others performed at the festival.


Also DJs: PhatPhillie, Odie, Venom, ...

The latest addition to the xSTatic festival is stand-up, where stand-up comedians from the "Spli'cka Scena" collective perform. We hope to watch and listen to some other stand-up comedians in the next editions.
